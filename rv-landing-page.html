Questions

Frequently asked

Is the report really free?

Yes. Your custom RV Demand Report is completely free with no obligation. You’ll see a preview instantly, and you can unlock and download the full report by entering your email.

How does weather-triggered advertising work?

We identify the weather moments that drive RV demand in your market — warm weekends, storms, heat, first frost, freeze warnings — and activate your ads to in-market buyers exactly when those conditions hit, pausing when they don’t.

Who will actually see my ads?

Households in your market that are actively shopping for RVs, layered with campground & state-park geotargeting and location look-back (people recently seen at campgrounds), across Connected TV, streaming radio, podcasts, display, and digital out-of-home.

Do I have to sign a long contract?

The SmartForecast is a month-to-month plan. Unused budget from slow-weather windows rolls forward as credit, so your media keeps working even when the weather doesn’t.

How fast can we launch?

Most dealers can launch within days. Start with the free report, and a Smart 1 strategist can review it and build your campaign from there.
